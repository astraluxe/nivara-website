Run AI on your own machine

adris.tech can use a model that lives on your computer instead of the cloud. Nothing leaves your laptop, there is no usage meter to watch, and it keeps working offline.

A local model can do everything the hosted AI does. Web lookups, Google Maps, LinkedIn scanning and file handling are performed by the app, not by the cloud — a local model drives exactly the same tools. What changes is size: writing and summaries run happily on a small model, while lookups, multi‑step jobs and research need a larger one. adris.tech checks your memory and free disk, then tells you which model fits.
