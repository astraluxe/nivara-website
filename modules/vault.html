00 What is Vault

Blocks harmful sites and trackers — before they reach your screen.

Works for every app on your device · on by default · free with every account

When you open a link, your device first looks up that address — basically asking "where is this site?" Vault sits in that lookup and checks every request against lists of known dangerous, ad-serving and tracking sites. If it's on the list, the request is blocked before anything loads. The ad never appears, the tracker never runs, the dangerous page never opens.

This is different from a browser ad-blocker: Vault works for every app on your device, not just your browser. It's on from the moment you install adris.tech — you don't have to configure anything.

02 Protection levels

Five levels — from gentle to locked-down.

Pick how aggressive Vault is. Most people leave it on the recommended level.

  1. SwiftBlocks only known-dangerous sites. Nothing breaks — maximum compatibility.
  2. Block — recommendedAdds ads and trackers on top of dangerous sites. The right setting for most people.
  3. GuardEverything in Block, plus stronger social-media tracking protection — good for sensitive business work.
  4. CoreMaximum blocking. A few sites may not load — best for confidential projects.
  5. FamilyBlocks everything except an approved list. Good for a shared home machine.
03 DNS privacy

Your network sees nothing. By design.

Every lookup is encrypted (DNS-over-HTTPS) — your ISP can't log where you go

Every site you visit starts with a lookup. Normally your ISP can see and log every one. Vault encrypts those lookups, so nobody on your network — not your ISP, not the café router — can see your browsing destinations. It's often faster than your default ISP lookup, too.

Bad domains, blocked before they load.

Checks every domain against malware & phishing lists — refreshed continuously
