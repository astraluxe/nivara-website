00 What is Brain

Your business, as a living graph.

One shared memory for you and your agents · saved as connected nodes · stored on your machine

Ask an AI to find leads today, and tomorrow it has no idea it ever did. Brain fixes that. Everything your Krew agents produce — company lists, verified contacts, research findings, outreach drafts — is saved as a node in a visual graph and connected to the files it came from. Attach your product doc, ask for buyers, and the resulting lead list is linked to that exact document. Next week, when you say "draft outreach for those companies," the agents recall the saved list instead of researching it all over again.

Real story — Solo founder in Bengaluru

A founder asked Krew to find buyers for his product, then came back three days later.

Before Brain, day two meant starting over: the agent had no memory of the list, so it researched the same companies again — spending the same time and the same AI budget twice.

With Brain, the verified lead list was already a node linked to his product file. When he said "draft outreach for these companies," the agents pulled the saved list, wrote the messages, and saved those as a connected node too. Follow-up work now starts from where the last session ended — not from zero.

01 How it works

Save. Link. Recall.

Agents write to the graph automatically · you can see and edit everything

You don't have to manage any of this. When agents do real work — building a lead list, drafting outreach, researching a company — the result is saved to the Brain automatically and linked to the files involved. You can also add your own notes and files, connect any two items, or edit what's stored. What the agents "remember" is visible to you, not hidden in a black box.

  1. Work gets capturedAttach a file or ask for research — the outputs are saved as nodes, automatically linked to their source.
  2. Knowledge gets connectedLead lists link to your product doc, outreach drafts link to the lead list — a real map of your business.
  3. Agents recall before they researchNext request, agents check the Brain first. Found it? They reuse it — no repeated searching, no repeated cost.
  4. You stay in controlPan, zoom, search and edit the graph. Add notes, connect items, or delete anything — it's your data, on your disk.
02 Why it matters

Memory is what turns a chatbot into a colleague.

Context survives · work compounds · tokens go further

The hidden cost of AI tools isn't the subscription — it's repetition. Re-explaining your business every session, re-running research the tool already did, re-building lists that existed last week. Brain removes that tax. Because agents recall saved knowledge instead of re-fetching it, the same monthly AI budget covers meaningfully more work — and long-running efforts like a sales pipeline actually accumulate progress per contact instead of resetting.

The bottom line: with Brain, your AI gets more useful the longer you use it — because it's building a private, editable memory of your business on your own machine, not starting fresh every conversation.
