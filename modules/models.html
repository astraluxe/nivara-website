00 What is Models

Run any AI model on your laptop — in two clicks, free.

Browse a catalogue · click install · adris.tech picks the right version for your machine

Hundreds of powerful AI models can be downloaded and used for free. The problem has always been the setup: figuring out which version fits your laptop's memory, installing extra software, configuring it, then wiring it into the tool you want to use.

Models hub removes all of that. You browse, pick, and click install. adris.tech auto-detects your RAM, downloads the right version, verifies it, and connects it to Krew and Coder automatically. The model runs on your own hardware — so you pay nothing per conversation.

03 Bring your own key

No GPU? Use the cloud.

Plug in your own OpenAI / Gemini / Claude key — at provider cost, zero markup

If your laptop can't run a big model, plug your own API key into Connect Apps. adris.tech routes Krew, Coder and Guard through your key at the provider's own price — no adris.tech markup. Your key, your bill, your data.
