00 What is Studio

Workflows that run themselves — even while you sleep.

Connect a trigger → add a Krew agent → connect an action. Runs on a schedule, forever.

Studio is where you build automations — sequences of steps that happen automatically, without you clicking anything. You've probably heard of Zapier or Make. Studio does the same job, except your AI agents from Krew are the workers inside each step, and everything runs on your laptop at no extra cost per run.

A workflow is three simple parts: something that starts it, an agent that does the thinking, and an action that delivers the result. You wire them together once on a canvas, and it keeps running every time its trigger fires.

One trigger fires three agents, pauses for your approval, then drops the result into Notion, email and Slack.
Real story — Freelance recruiter

Arjun does freelance tech recruitment in Pune, juggling 4–5 open roles at a time.

Every new batch of applications meant 3–4 hours reading CVs, deciding who moves forward, writing rejection emails, and making notes on the shortlist — several times a week. It was burning half his working hours.

He built one Studio workflow. Every morning at 9 AM it runs on its own: checks his email for new CVs, passes each to a Krew agent that already knows the job brief, scores and sorts each candidate, drafts personal rejections, and creates Notion cards for the shortlist. Arjun opens his laptop at 9:15 and it's already done — 15 minutes of review instead of 4 hours of work.

Drag triggers and actions from the sidebar; wire them on the grid. That's the whole builder.
  1. A trigger starts itA time ("every day at 9 AM"), a new email, a form submission, a webhook, or an API call — you choose what fires the workflow.
  2. A Krew agent does the thinkingRead this, summarise that, decide between options, write a reply — the AI handles the judgement in the middle of the flow.
  3. An action delivers the resultSend an email, update Notion, write to a Google Sheet, post to Slack. Studio drops the finished result where it belongs.
