Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026 · Effective immediately · Bengaluru, India

adris.tech ("Adris", "we", "us", or "our") operates the adris.tech website and the Adris desktop application (the "App"). This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We've written it in plain language — not lawyer-speak.

1. Who we are

adris.tech is a software product built and operated from Bengaluru, India. Our primary contact for privacy matters is adris.tech/partner — use type "Contact / Other" and include "Privacy" in the description.

2. Data we collect

2a. Account data

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password (managed by Supabase Auth). We do not collect your name unless you provide it.

2b. App usage data

The Adris desktop app stores the following entirely on your device — it is never sent to our servers unless you explicitly use a cloud feature:

  • Agent conversations and memory
  • Automation workflow configurations
  • Code projects and file paths
  • Vault (DNS) settings and blocklist preferences
  • API keys you enter via Connect Apps (stored in your OS keychain, never transmitted)
  • Downloaded AI model files

2c. Cloud feature data

If you use adris.tech Cloud AI (our server-side model inference), we process your message through our Supabase Edge Function and log token usage to enforce plan limits. We do not store message content beyond the immediate request/response cycle.

2d. Payment data

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We never see or store your card number, UPI PIN, or bank credentials. We receive a payment confirmation and subscription status from Razorpay's webhook.

2e. Website analytics

Our website may use privacy-respecting analytics (no cookies, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking). We collect only aggregate page view counts and referral sources.

2f. Affiliate applications

If you apply for our affiliate program, we store your name, email, website URL, audience description, and promotion plan. This data is used only to review your application and manage the affiliate relationship.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the App and website
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, payment receipts, affiliate payouts)
  • To enforce plan limits and prevent abuse
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the product (using aggregate, anonymised patterns only)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Data sharing

We share data only with the following sub-processors, strictly for the purpose of operating the service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (hosted in AWS ap-south-1, Mumbai)
  • Razorpay — payment processing (India-based, RBI-regulated)
  • Google (Gemini API) — AI inference for adris.tech Cloud mode only
  • Cloudflare R2 — file storage for uploaded assets (optional feature)

We do not share data with any advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party analytics companies.

5. Data retention

  • Account data: retained until you delete your account
  • Token usage logs: retained for 90 days for billing verification
  • Affiliate application data: retained for 3 years from the date of application
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required by Indian GST law

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data (email us to request)
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request via adris.tech/partner with the description "Data Request". We respond within 7 business days.

7. Cookies

The Adris desktop app does not use cookies. The website uses strictly necessary cookies only (session state for the theme toggle and tour progress). No third-party tracking cookies are set.

8. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+. Data at rest in Supabase is AES-256 encrypted. API keys you enter in the App are stored in your operating system's native keychain (Windows Credential Manager), not in our database. We perform regular security audits and notify affected users within 72 hours of a confirmed data breach.

9. Children's privacy

Adris is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, email us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We will notify you of material changes by email (if you have an account) and by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Contact: adris.tech/partner — use type "Contact / Other"
  • Include "Privacy" or "Data Request" in your description
  • Response time: within 7 business days
