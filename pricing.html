Common questions

What's the difference between BYOK and managed plans?

With BYOK (Bring Your Own Key), you connect your own Gemini, OpenAI, or Claude API key — you pay the AI provider directly and adris.tech charges only a small platform fee (₹500/mo or $5/mo). Managed plans mean adris.tech provides the AI — no key needed, tokens are included in the plan price.

What happens when I hit my monthly token limit?

The app shows a friendly notice and switches you to BYOK mode automatically — you can continue working using your own API key with zero interruption. Your data and history stay intact. Upgrade anytime to get more managed tokens.

What counts as an "automation run"?

Each time an automation you've saved runs — whether triggered by a schedule, incoming email, or file drop — that counts as one run. Creating and editing automations doesn't use runs; only live execution does.

Can I use a local model (Ollama, LM Studio) on any plan?

Yes — local models work on every plan including BYOK. They don't consume managed tokens or automation run counts since everything stays on your machine.

Is there a free plan?

Yes — the Explore tier gives you 100,000 managed tokens (lifetime, not monthly) to try the platform at no cost. It's available the moment you download adris.tech. No credit card required.

Do BYOK plans get discounts for longer commitments?

Yes. Pay quarterly for 10% off, semi-annually for 15% off, or annually for the best rate. You can lock in at ₹450/mo (vs ₹500/mo) on the 3-month BYOK plan, all the way down to ₹267/mo on the annual plan.
