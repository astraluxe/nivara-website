// 03 — Private by architecture

Your data never leaves the building.

Most AI platforms run your business through their cloud. adris runs on your hardware — the same guarantee you'd demand from any system that touches client data.

■ Your machine
🔒 Conversations — local SQLite database
🔒 Credentials — encrypted on-device store
🔒 Lead lists & documents — local knowledge graph
🔒 AI models — run offline via Ollama
✕
cloud sync — off by default · only what you explicitly enable
  • ✓Local-first storage. Chat history, automations and your knowledge graph live in SQLite files on your disk — not on our servers.
  • ✓Credentials stay on-device. Gmail, Notion, Slack tokens are stored in an encrypted local store. API calls go directly from your machine to the service.
  • ✓Bring your own key. Connect your own Gemini, OpenAI or Claude key — AI calls go straight from your device to the provider.
  • ✓Network-level protection included. Vault blocks trackers and malicious domains at the DNS level; Guard scans contracts and monitors threats — all computed locally.
