What we're open to

Three ways to partner

We're a small team. We respond to every serious request — no corporate gatekeeping.

01 · MCP & Tools Showcase your MCP or tool

Built an MCP server, VS Code extension, CLI tool, or API that developers and builders would love? Get it in front of adris.tech users directly inside the app — in the MCP marketplace or as a featured integration in Krew.

02 · Service Exchange Give ↔ receive services

Your company offers something we need (design, legal, infra, cloud credits) — and in return you get adris.tech licenses, integrations, or co-marketing. Mutual value, no middlemen. Common with SaaS companies and agencies.

03 · Business Proposal Collaborate commercially

Resell our platform to your customers, embed adris.tech in your product, co-market to your audience, or propose an investment/acquisition conversation. If it creates value for Indian builders, we want to hear it.

04 · Contact / Other Reach out directly

Press, feedback, complaints, integrations that don't fit the above, or anything else. This is also the primary contact channel for adris.tech — we read every submission. Response time: 24–48 hours.
