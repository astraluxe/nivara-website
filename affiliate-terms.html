5. Commission Structure

You earn Commission for each calendar month in which you have Active Paying Referrals. Commission is calculated as follows:

  • Starter tier (1–10 active referrals): 10% of Net Payment Amount per Active Paying Referral.
  • Builder tier (11–30 active referrals): 13% of Net Payment Amount per Active Paying Referral.
  • Partner tier (31–99 active referrals): 16% of Net Payment Amount per Active Paying Referral.
  • Elite tier (100+ active referrals): 20% of Net Payment Amount per Active Paying Referral.

Tier upgrades and downgrades happen automatically at the start of each calendar month based on the number of Active Paying Referrals in the previous month. Commission for a given month is calculated at the tier applicable at the beginning of that month.

Commission is earned on recurring monthly plan payments only. One-time purchases, add-ons, and refunded transactions do not generate Commission. If a Referred User cancels their plan, Commission ceases the month following cancellation.
